Clarinet
--------

Version 2019-08-18

Clarinet samples by Tyler Dence <tyzoid.d@gmail.com> (Recording Engineer) and
Kaili Dence (Performer), contributed to the FreePats project on August 2017.
Recorded with an AudioTechnica AT873R microphone, placed a foot and a half
away from the bell of the instrument, angled towards the middle section.

Sound bank, loops and modifications to the samples by Roberto
<roberto@zenvoid.org>.

This sound bank, in several formats and qualities, is available on the
FreePats project web pages: http://freepats.zenvoid.org/Reed/clarinet.html
